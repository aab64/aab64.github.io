Astrid Boje

I am a postdoc in Chemical Physics at Chalmers University of Technology in Sweden. My research focus is on modelling chemical reaction kinetics and transport in nanoscale, heterogeneous catalysis. I have a PhD in Chemical Engineering from the University of Cambridge, where I worked in the Computational Modelling (CoMo) group with stochastic methods for solving population balance equations for aerosol synthesis.
